About

A builder who believes in the dignity of good work

My name is Chaiwat — known to most simply as Ton. I hold a degree in Computer Engineering and spent fifteen years as a Project Manager, navigating the quiet complexity of Banking, Telecommunications, and Government transformation.

Today, I lead UP Wellness — a health and wellness venture focused on weight loss and sustainable wellbeing, built in partnership with Amway Thailand. Alongside it, I am building a new chapter: an AI-powered business that uses agentic tools to create value, automate intelligently, and reclaim time.

I am calm by nature and deliberate by practice. I believe that freedom is not a reward for finishing — it is the foundation from which good work begins.

Philosophy

On freedom, work, and the long game

I came to understand early that money, in itself, is not the destination — it is the vehicle. What I am truly building toward is a life of freedom: the freedom to move, to choose, to create without asking permission.

"Passive income is the only income that buys back time. And time, unlike capital, cannot be compounded — only reclaimed."

I simplify by instinct. Every system I touch, I reduce to its essential form. Every venture I build, I ask: does this create value that outlasts my direct involvement? That question is my compass.
